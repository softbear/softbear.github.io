Q&A

Q: What should I bring with me?
A: a wrapped gift valued at under $25.

Q: Why do I need to bring a gift? Are we doing a secret santa?
A:We are doing a variation of secret santa, called the White Elephant. You don’t know who is getting your gift, so feel free to express your creative and mischievous sides when choosing a gift. For game rules and strategies, please see https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/White_elephant_gift_exchange

Q: I heard there is a talent show? OMG, I’m nervous now.
A: Don’t be nervous, we think you are all super talented. Lucas and Ryan have been nominated to show off their musical prowess. If you have a talent you would like to demonstrate, we would love to be entertained by you. Otherwise, this is a great opportunity for a quick “Getting to know me” presentation, even if you have done one already.

Q: Is dinner included? There better be free food if you expect me to show up.
A: Of course! The exact menu will be a surprise, but rest assured that there will be vegetarian foods and non-alcoholic drinks, as well as non-vegetarian foods and non-non-alcoholic drinks. If you have any dietary restrictions, please let Sheri and Robin know.

Q: Can I bring a +1? (Or +2 since the food is free).
A: Of course! We want to get to know you and those who are close and dear to you. We encourage you to encourage your +1 to bring a gift too so they can participate in our white elephant gift exchange. If your +1 is of the canine species, please notify our Beloved hosts, who have two lovely canines of their own. Please let Sheri and Robin know how many +1 you will be bringing, and we will make sure there is enough food, refreshments, and holiday cheer to go around.

Q: Is parking included?
A: No, but you may find empty spots along the side of the street suitable for parking.

Q: I only chug X-brand of alcohol.
A: That’s not really a question, but in that case you are expected to bring some to share at the party.

Q: I don’t believe in Santa, is that ok?
A: No, it's not ok, you can clearly see that Santa visits everyone using the official Santa Tracker: http://www.noradsanta.org/

Q: I’m not much of a party animal, would I fit in?
A: Yes, we welcome equally party plants, party fungi, party protists, party bacteria, and party archaea.

Q: Who should I direct further questions to?
A: Sheri and Robin.
